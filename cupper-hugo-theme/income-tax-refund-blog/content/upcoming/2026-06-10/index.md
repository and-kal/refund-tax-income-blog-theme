»**Nina Dalva** improvises electroacoustic soundscapes & songs which take shapes based on her voice or sampled voices. Sometimes acapella, sometimes overdubbed or fakely so. She plays and replays polyphonic picked texts, fake folk genre & field recordings: composed as signified images & metaphor, with which she seats on the floor to have a closer look.«

[soundcloud](https://soundcloud.com/mur3n3)

»For over ten years *Apolline Schöser* and *Nocola Henry* have been composing songs in multiple languages and experimenting with sound through collages, contrasts, and little sonic tricks. Their live shows take a very different approach — they explore staging as much as the silences between guitar notes, so that from their gentle acoustic melodies can emerge sudden, amplified bursts. On this tour, **Nina Harker** will present a minimal, contrasted live show between concert and performance, attentive to space, bodies and sound textures.«

[nina harker](https://aguirrerecords.bandcamp.com/album/nina-harker) (2023 album on Aguirre Records)
