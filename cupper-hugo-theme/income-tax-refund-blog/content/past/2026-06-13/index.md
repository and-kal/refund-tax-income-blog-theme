**Elizabeth Vogler**

Liturgical harmonium dreamstates and ethereal vocal reverences fill the air around the monks, church cleaners, pastoralists and you.

[AU NOM DEUS ASOS](https://billotontraeger.bandcamp.com/album/elizabeth-vogler-au-nom-deus-asos) (_2025 album on Billo_)

> Elizabeth Vogler creates immersive soundscapes that blend voice, drones, electroacoustic textures, and electronic experimentation. Moving between traditional-inspired composition and glitch-infused ritual, her music unfolds through improvisation into a rich, organic whole.

**Nina Dalva**

[soundcloud](https://soundcloud.com/mur3n3)
